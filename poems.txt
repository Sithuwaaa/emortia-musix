HOW TO ADD A POEM
-----------------
Everything above the first "@title:" line is ignored, so these notes stay private.

To publish a new poem, copy the block below and change the words:

    @title: Your poem title
    @date: July 2026
    First line of the poem.
    Second line.

    A blank line just makes a stanza break — it is kept exactly as you type it.

Rules:
  - "@title:" starts a new poem (this is required).
  - "@date:" is optional — write anything you like (a month, a full date, a mood).
  - Newest poem at the top shows first on the site.
  - Save the file, commit, and it appears on the Journal page automatically.

Delete this whole note block and the sample poem below once you add your own.


@title: How to add a poem
@date: July 2026
This is a sample entry so you can see how a poem looks on the page.

Replace it with your own words.
Each line breaks exactly where you break it,

and a blank line opens a little space
the way a breath does between two thoughts.
