About

Emortia is a producer and sound designer who never quite picked a lane. Half the week is spent writing music; the other half is spent running the small machine that lets the music exist — invoices, decks, deadlines, the lot.

This site is both halves in one place: a player for the work, and a workspace for everything around it. Built warm, kept honest.
